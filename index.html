ABOUT US

Alexis Law is a young and dynamic Family and Estate Law firm located in Richmond, British Columbia. We focus on all aspects of Family and Estate Law, and understand that family and estate matters can be legally and emotionally challenging and complex. It is our goal to help you through the process with the most efficient and affordable legal representation possible.

Our Approach

We are, first and foremost, good listeners.

We are experts in the 2 L’s of legal services - Listening and Law. We offer initial consultations where we patiently listen to your concerns and issues, before we let our legal expertise drive the conversation forward.

We are efficient.

At Alexis Law, we understand that legal matters can be both time consuming and financially stressful. We are mindful of this, and strive for maximum efficiency in our practice to keep your legal costs affordable.

We make the law accessible.

Many individuals choose to self-represent during a legal proceeding, however, they still need legal assistance from time to time. In those instances, we offer unbundled legal services on an as-needed basis. Whether you need assistance drafting a complex document, representation at a single court hearing, or simply guidance to point in the right direction, it’s all in a day’s work for us.

We keep it simple.

Just like we are efficient in terms of costs, we are economical with words too. You will find that our communication, whether written or spoken, belongs in the 21st century, not in the 19th. We guarantee that communication received from us will not send you running for the thesaurus. We speak English, not legalese. (Having said that, we do speak Hindi and Sindhi as well!)
